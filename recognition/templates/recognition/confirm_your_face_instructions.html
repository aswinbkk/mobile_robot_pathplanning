Instructions

1. The webcam, which is used for object tracking, isn’t covered by anything, be it a screen protector, case or even dirt, etc.
2. Since the object tracking feature works by scanning given map, ensure it’s visible to the webcam.
3. Try to use the feature in environments that are well-lit in order to get the best results.
4. When outside on a sunny day, make sure the webcam in a way that the sun and the scanner are not in direct contact.
5. To get the best from object tracking, make sure webcam is in landscape orientation, also ensure that you map the webcam keep closer, in best length.
6. Ensure that Firebird V has a sufficient battery to run.
7. Place the gripper arm in the relaxed position.
8. Properly place Firebird V in the grid(1,1).

Procedure

1. Press START button for object tracking process.
2. Wait few seconds to begin the process.
3. After tracking process, you will redirect to Path selection process, is it failure read our instructions then repeat these process again.
4. Finally mobile robot move through selected path.
