Instructions

1. The webcam, which is used for facial recognition, isn’t covered by anything, be it a screen protector, case or even dirt, etc.
2. Since the face unlock feature works by scanning your face (eyes, nose and mouth), ensure it’s visible to the webcam.
3. Removing the glasses always help with Face unlock, there are some sunglasses that block light and thus may prevent the face unlock feature from working properly.
4. Try to use the feature in environments that are well-lit in order to get the best results.
5. When outside on a sunny day, make sure the webcam in a way that the sun and the scanner are not in direct contact.
6. To get the best from face unlock, make sure webcam is in portrait orientation. As noted earlier, also ensure that you face the webcam and keep your webcam closer to your face, best length.

Procedure

1. Press START button for face authentication process.
2. Wait few seconds to begin the process.
3. After authentication process, you will redirect to Home page, is it failure read our instructions then repeat these process again.
